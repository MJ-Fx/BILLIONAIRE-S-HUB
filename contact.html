Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the mentorship program?

Simply reach out to us through any of our contact channels, and we'll guide you through the onboarding process. We'll discuss your trading goals and match you with the right mentor.

What is the cost of the mentorship program?

We offer different mentorship tiers to suit various budgets and commitment levels. Contact us for detailed pricing information based on your specific needs.

Do I need prior trading experience?

No, we welcome traders at all experience levels. Our mentors will tailor the program to your current knowledge and goals, whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced trader looking to refine your strategy.

How often will I interact with my mentor?

This depends on the program you choose. Most programs include weekly 1-on-1 sessions, with additional support available through our community platform between sessions.
